I am a Ph.D. student in cotutelle between LaBRI , Université de Bordeaux and Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI). My advisors are Igor Walukiewicz and Frédéric Herbreteau at LaBRI and B Srivathsan at CMI. My research is in the field of formal verification, with a focus on developing efficient methods for the verification of networks of timed automata.
