I am a faculty member at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences(IMSc), Chennai, India. My research is in formal methods, where, in particular, I am interested in automata theory, logic, and automated synthesis, and its application in the formal verification.

Before this, I was a Postdoc in the Algorithmic Verification group at the Department of Information Technology at Uppsala University, where I work with Parosh Abdulla and Mohamed Faouzi Atig. Before that, I was a post-doctoral research fellow with Krishna S and S Akshay at IIT Bombay.

Prior to that, I was a Ph.D. student at both LaBRI, Université de Bordeaux and Chennai Mathematical Institute (CMI). My advisors were Igor Walukiewicz and Frédéric Herbreteau at LaBRI and B Srivathsan at CMI. Before that, I was a master’s student at Chennai Mathematical Institute. Even before that, I obtained the degree of B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Calicut.
